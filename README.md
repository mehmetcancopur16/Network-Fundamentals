## 🚀 The Vision

You cannot hack or protect a system if you do not understand how data travels through it. Networking is the absolute bedrock of Information Security, Penetration Testing, and Systems Engineering.

This repository is designed to take you from **Zero to Hero**. It strips away the unnecessary fluff and focuses on the core mechanics of how devices communicate, how packets are routed, and how physical signals are transmitted. Whether you are prepping for certifications (like CCNA, CompTIA Network+, or CEH) or building your Red/Blue team foundation, this repo is your ultimate reference guide.

## 🛠️ How to Use This Repository

1. **Read Sequentially:** The concepts build upon each other. Start from Chapter 01 and work your way down.
2. **Practice the Commands:** Open your terminal (`cmd`, `PowerShell`, or `bash`) and try the troubleshooting commands in Chapter 07 on your local network.
3. **Understand the Math:** Pay special attention to **Chapter 02**. Subnetting is a mathematical skill that requires practice. Grab a pen and paper and calculate the subnets manually.
